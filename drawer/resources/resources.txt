<p>Here you can find links to Christian resources which we have found beneficial to our own walks. If you are not a Christian, please do not hesitate to also browse these links and read through <a href="../drawer.php?drawer=gospel">The Gospel</a>.</p>

<h2><u><a href="http://lighthousebc.com/beacon/">The Beacon</a></u></h2>
<p>The Beacon is the online newsletter for Lighthouse Bible Church San Diego. It is frequently updated with articles that cover a variety of topics and current events from a Christian perspective. If you ever feel the need to research a Christian topic, but don't know which sources to trust on Google search, feel free to give Beacon author, Stephen Rogers' customized search engine, <a href="https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/12715014/stoogle_lbc.html">Stoogle</a>, a go.</p>

<h2><u><a href="http://www.albertmohler.com/">Albert Mohler</a></u></h2>
<p>Southern Baptist Theological Seminary president, Albert Mohler maintains a blog covering a vast number topics from a Christian worldview from A-Z (W actually). <a href="http://www.albertmohler.com/category/the-briefing/">The Briefing</a> contains daily audio podcasts from Dr. Mohler himself, covering daily news headlines and current events worldwide.</p>

<h2><u><a href="http://www.gty.org/">Grace To You</a></u></h2>
<p>Grace To You contains a whole repository of sermons from Pastor John MacArthur's Grace Community Church. You can also find daily radio broadcasts by Pastor MacArthur as well as blog posts, devotionals, and even use their online shop for books and commentaries. If you need a Bible to-go, feel free to download their <a href="http://www.gtybibleapp.com/">Grace To You Bible App</a>.</p>

<h2><u><a href="http://www.handlettering.co/">Hand Lettering Co.</a></u></h2>
<p>Hand Lettering Co. offers some of the most aesthically pleasing Christian typography with the goal of encouraging and aiding in Bible memorization, all for the glory of God. Their hand lettered art pieces range from scriptural verses to Christian quotes, all available in a variety of poster sizes suitable for your home or office space. Feel free to also visit their <a href="http://www.handlettering.co/encourage/">Encourage</a> page, which offers a variety of wallpapers, free for download, for your desktop, tablet, or phone.</p>
